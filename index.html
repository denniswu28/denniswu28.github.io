Density-Field Forecasting with High-Redshift Uncertainty

Arizona Cosmology Lab · Research Assistant · Advisor: Professor Tim Eifler

I am leading a study of baryon acoustic oscillation reconstruction under photometric-redshift smearing. I built a 250-cell sensitivity grid and a modeling pipeline that reduced reconstruction bias by a factor of six. The project combines simulation, uncertainty propagation, and statistical validation and is being prepared as a first-author paper.

I presented the work in the cosmology session at the SPHEREx Science Team Face-to-Face E4 meeting at IPAC, Caltech.
