Density-Field Forecasting with High-Redshift Uncertainty

Arizona Cosmology Lab, University of Arizona · Research Assistant · January 2026 to Present

  • Project Leadership. Leading a first-author study of baryon acoustic oscillation reconstruction under photometric-redshift smearing, independently designing the analysis and coordinating with the PI and a postdoctoral collaborator across the full pipeline, from simulated-catalog generation through Bayesian posterior inference. A first-author publication is in preparation.
  • Forward Modeling. Building a forward-model framework that propagates photometric-redshift uncertainty through BAO reconstruction to quantify its effect on standard-ruler calibration, a leading systematic for next-generation surveys including Roman and Rubin.
  • Systematics Control. Diagnosed and corrected a systematic bias in the Bayesian MCMC inference pipeline by cross-validating two independent covariance-estimation methods across a 250-cell parameter sensitivity grid, reducing the bias by four to six times.
  • Sensitivity Analysis. Mapped the smearing amplitude at which reconstruction's benefit decays from a 20 to 40 percent precision gain to statistical insignificance using Monte Carlo simulation over a 25-realization ensemble.
